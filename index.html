Origin Story

In 1976, Founder Pho Kim Long arrived in America. He started H-Thai-Ml cooking in his garage on New Orleans, where his first restaurant is located. More than 40 years later, it has blossomed into three restaurants serving only the best Thai cuisine from Bangkok.
